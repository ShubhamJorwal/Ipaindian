Voting Pattern Analysis

Historical Trends:
• Tumkur City has been a competitive constituency with alternating victories between BJP and Congress
• BJP has won the last two consecutive elections (2018, 2023) with G.B. Jyothi Ganesh
• The constituency has a significant Muslim population (24.5%) which traditionally influences voting patterns
• Voter turnout has been consistently above 65% in recent elections
• JD(S) has emerged as a strong third force, finishing second in both 2018 and 2023
• The winning margin has been relatively narrow in recent elections, indicating a highly competitive seat
