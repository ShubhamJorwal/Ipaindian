Political Career & Background

Political Journey

Thammesh Gowda has been an active member of the Bharatiya Janata Party since 2010. He started his political career as a booth-level worker and gradually rose through the ranks due to his dedication and grassroots connect. He served as the Ward President of Byatarayanapura from 2015-2018 and as the Mandal President from 2018-2023.

Social Work & Community Service

Before entering active politics, Thammesh has been involved in various social welfare activities including free education programs for underprivileged children, health camps, and skill development initiatives. He founded "Yuva Shakti Foundation" in 2012 which has trained over 5,000 youth in various skills.

Vision for Constituency

His primary focus areas include infrastructure development, improved water supply, better healthcare facilities, quality education, and employment opportunities for youth. He aims to transform Byatarayanapura into a model constituency with world-class infrastructure while preserving its cultural heritage.
